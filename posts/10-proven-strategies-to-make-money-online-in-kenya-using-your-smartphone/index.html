From freelance gigs to eCommerce, there are plenty of ways to start and grow a profitable online venture. So, if you’re ready to take the plunge into the world of online money-making, here are 10 proven strategies to make money online in Kenya using your smartphone.

  1. Freelance Writing: If you have a way with words, then freelance writing should be one of your go-to strategies. A variety of publications offer freelance writing opportunities in Kenya, where you can earn money for your writing. Even better, you don’t need to leave the comfort of your home to start making money from freelance writing. All you need is a good laptop and reliable internet connection and you’re good to go.

  2. Social Media Management: If you’ve got a knack for social media, then you can use it to make money online. You can find freelance gigs, where clients pay you to manage their social media accounts and manage their online reputation. You’ll need to have an understanding of the main social media platforms, such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram in order to make a success of this venture.

  3. Online Tutoring: If you have an expertise in a particular field, then you can offer your services as an online tutor. You can advertise your skills on various websites and use your smartphone to connect with clients who are looking for tuition services.

  4. Selling Crafts: If you have the skills to make beautiful crafts, then you can make money by selling them online. You can create a website or an online store, and use social media to promote and advertise your products.

  5. Affiliate Marketing: As an affiliate marketer, you can earn commission by promoting other people’s products. All you have to do is find a product that you’re passionate about and then promote it on your website or blog. You’ll earn a commission every time someone purchases the product through your link.

  6. Selling Stock Photos: If you have a keen eye for detail, then you can make money by selling stock photos online. You can upload your photos to popular stock photo websites and then wait for people to purchase them.

  7. Copywriting: If you have a way with words, then you can make money by writing copy for websites and other online publications. You can find copywriting gigs on freelance websites.

  8. Creating an Online Course: If you’ve got expertise in a particular topic, then you can create an online course and make money from it. You can create a course on your smartphone using video editing apps, and then set up a website to host the course.

  9. Dropshipping: If you’re looking to start an eCommerce business, then dropshipping is one of the quickest and easiest ways to start. You can find products to sell on dropshipping websites and then link them to your online store. You’ll never have to worry about inventory as the supplier will deliver the products directly to your customers.

  10. Flipping Items: If you have an eye for a bargain, then you can make money by flipping items. You can purchase items from online retailers and then resell them at a higher price. It’s a great way to make money with minimal effort.

So, if you’re looking for ways to make money online in Kenya using your smartphone, then you should definitely consider these 10 proven strategies. With a bit of hard work and dedication, you can make a success of any of these money-making ventures. Good luck!
